# Code Of Conduct

This organization expects respectful, direct, and professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus feedback on the work.
- Share concrete evidence for technical claims.
- Respect repo sensitivity boundaries and private data.
- Escalate disagreements with specifics, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Doxxing, secret leakage, or sharing sensitive internal context outside approved channels.
- Repeated low-signal disruption after feedback.

## Reporting

Security-sensitive or conduct-sensitive issues should be reported through the process in `SECURITY.md`.
